Percheron

Next horse to be added into the game is Percheron!
You can choose between: Bay Pangare, Black, Dapple Grey, Flaxen Chestnut, Light Grey and Steel Grey. (Seal brown will be added later)
Percheron has a special gait "moose walk", which reminds Curly Suffle and Icelandic Tölt.
The cost for one horse is 850SC.
